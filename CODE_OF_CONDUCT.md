# Code of Conduct

## Pledge
We are committed to making participation in the CNMRF community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
*   Using welcoming and inclusive language
*   Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
*   Gracefully accepting constructive criticism

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project author.
